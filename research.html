Research

The Visualization and Graphics Lab (VGL) provides many ways for students to participate in research, including paid research assistant positions, directed studies (CS 498/698), sponsored projects (CS 490/690), visualization-focused research seminars (CS 384/684), special topics courses in visualization (CS 486/686), and dedicated visualization-focused undergraduate and graduate courses (CS 360, MSAN 622).
